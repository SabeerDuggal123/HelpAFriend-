Physical Health

Physical health encompasses both a healthy body and a healthy mind. There are two important components that can help ensure a healthy body â€“ physical activity and diet. Hence to ensure that we keep ourselves at our healthy best physically we need to focus on the following:

  • Physical Activity

    Physical activity is very important to ensure that our body stays in best condition, reduces the risk of disease and gives us energy and positivity. Yoga is an old and effective way of exercise. It tones our body without undue pressure to our bones and other body parts. It is proven to provide best results when done consistently over a period of time. However, we need to ensure that we have learnt how to do the asanas well before we attempt them since if done improperly it can lead to more harm than good. You should try to get atleast 30 minutes of exercise daily.

  • Diet

    A good diet is an important part of physical health. We should ensure that our diet is balanced and adequate. Over-eating or binging of anything is very harmful hence portion control is very important. There is a growing trend these days to eat junk food, especially amongst the younger generation. Such food does not provide the necessary nutrients and minerals and often leads to the problem of over-weight which leads to diseases like diabetes and hypertension.

  • Healthy mind

    A healthy mind is just as important for our physical health as a healthy body. Mental well-being can be enhanced by various ways the most commonly practiced being meditation. Meditation is about mindfulness. It is the practice of training your mind to focus on a particular object and clearing away all other thoughts and activities. It gives you mental clarity and makes you emotionally calm. Regular meditation ensures a healthy mind free of stress and diseases like depression.
