Cyber-Security & Cyber-Bullying in COVID-19 times

Cyber-Security

Cyber-security is the practice of protection of hardware and software to ensure there is no misuse or information. With the growing use of computers and smartphones for the purpose of education cyber-security has become a real issue with the children. In this year of COVID-19. Most children have experienced it in some form or the other even when it is seemingly harmless like strangers entering the Zoom classrooms and causing disruption. However, it can have serious repercussions too and hence it is important to educate our children to ensure they are adequately protected.



Cyber-Bullying

Cyber-bullying is another growing trend that is a cause of great concern. Though everyone is exposed to cyber-bullying and has a negative impact due to it, the children are the most vulnerable. They are at an impressionable age and need to be protected.
